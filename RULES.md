# PRATHAMONE ACADEMY OS — GOVERNANCE MANIFEST

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The PrathamOne Academy OS is a **Sovereign Kernel**. All governance is modular and strictly enforced. Developers and AI Agents MUST read the relevant rule-books before commencing any task.

## Governance Structure

The OS governance is divided into a Core Constitution and domain-specific Module Rule-Books.

### 1. [Core Constitution](file:///d:/IMP/GitHub/prathamone-academy-os/RULE-BOOK/00_CORE_CONSTITUTION.md)
The non-negotiable **LOCKED v3.1 laws** (Laws 1–15) that govern the entire OS kernel.

### 2. Module Rule-Books
Specific laws and constraints for individual OS modules:

*   **[01 Admissions Rules](file:///d:/IMP/GitHub/prathamone-academy-os/RULE-BOOK/01_ADMISSIONS_RULES.md):** Student Core & lifecycle laws.
*   **[02 Exams Rules](file:///d:/IMP/GitHub/prathamone-academy-os/RULE-BOOK/02_EXAMS_RULES.md):** Marks, grading, and derivation logic.
*   **[03 Fees & Finance Rules](file:///d:/IMP/GitHub/prathamone-academy-os/RULE-BOOK/03_FEES_FINANCE_RULES.md):** Irreversible transaction & ledger laws.
*   **[04 Library Rules](file:///d:/IMP/GitHub/prathamone-academy-os/RULE-BOOK/04_LIBRARY_RULES.md):** Asset management & circulation laws.
*   **[05 Studybuddy AI Rules](file:///d:/IMP/GitHub/prathamone-academy-os/RULE-BOOK/05_STUDYBUDDY_AI_RULES.md):** Student safe-AI protocols (SB-1 to SB-12).
*   **[06 Report & Analytics Rules](file:///d:/IMP/GitHub/prathamone-academy-os/RULE-BOOK/06_REPORT_ANALYTICS_RULES.md):** Declarative extraction & visualization laws.
*   **[07 UI Metadata Rules](file:///d:/IMP/GitHub/prathamone-academy-os/RULE-BOOK/07_UI_METADATA_RULES.md):** Metadata-driven display and decoupling laws (UI-1 to UI-14).
*   **[08 Sovereign Rule Matrix](file:///d:/IMP/GitHub/prathamone-academy-os/RULE-BOOK/08_SOVEREIGN_RULE_MATRIX.md):** 🔒 Comprehensive cross-reference of all 59 Locked Laws across Layers 0–12 (Audit Output v1.0).
*   **[09 Pending Laws & Gap Report](file:///d:/IMP/GitHub/prathamone-academy-os/RULE-BOOK/09_PENDING_LAWS.md):** ⚠️ Vulnerability Gap Analysis — 6 critical gaps identified, 22 Draft Laws pending ratification.
